Improving Road Safety Using Camera Violation and Crash Data

Motivation

Police Deployment


Traffic rule enforcement is one of core duties of the Police Department. However, the police resource is limited, how to make efficient deployment to improve road safety is a key question. This project provide a tool to forecast violation risks at various locations throughout the city. By placing police officers at selected locations associated with higher violation risk, it is expected to promote the change to a good-driving behavior, to discover underlying reason for the violation, and to avoid the waiting period of a mailed ticket.


City Planning


Cameras are usually installed by the Department of City Planning. Installation at a major intersection or on a busy street is a common choice. How to smartly select a camera installation site is difficult to achieve. By combing the crash record together with the information of current camera sites, I derived a data-driven approach to suggest next camera installation sites and evaluate impacts of newly installed cameras on crash risks.

Data Source

Camera-recorded Traffic Violation Data

Thanks to the vast data from the city of Chicago. Both red light violations and speeding daily violations data for the time period July 2014 - Aug 2017 can be found online.

Motor Vehcile Crash Data

The Illinoise Deparment of Transportation's Division of Traffic Safety just released their records on crash accidents happedn in 2015. The data was posted in April, 2017.

Weather Data

The climate of Chicago is classified as humid continental, with all four seasons distinctly represented: wet, cool springs; somewhat hot, and often humid, summers; pleasantly mild autumns; and cold winters. Annual precipitation in Chicago is average, and reaches its lowest points in the months of January and February, and peaks in the months of May and June. Rich historical weather data can be found on the website of National Centers for Environmental Information.

Data Cleaning

Geocode

Although the address of each camera is provided, the information on corresponding latitude and longtitude is missing. The Google Map Geocoding API was applied to collect such information for better geographic mapping visulization.
